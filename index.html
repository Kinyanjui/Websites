Lower Kabete Two Bedroom all en suite, modern design, amenities
and key features

  • Sufficient Water and Power Supply
    with a Backup Generator

    The apartment is equipped with a reliable and ample supply of water and power to ensure that residents have uninterrupted access to these essential services.

    Water is sourced from multiple sources, including a borehole, which provides a steady supply of clean water even during periods of water shortages. The building also has an underground storage tank that stores large quantities of water to ensure a constant supply of water for residents. In addition to the borehole and storage tank, the apartment has overhead water tanks that provide additional storage and ensure a constant supply of water even during times of high demand.

    Similarly, the apartment has a reliable and uninterrupted power supply, with a stand-by generator as a backup power source. This ensures that residents have access to power even during periods of power outages or load shedding. The generator is designed to automatically kick in as soon as the main power supply goes out, ensuring that there is no disruption to residents' daily activities.

  • Security and Property Management
    Office

    The apartment has been designed with security in mind, providing residents with peace of mind knowing that their safety is a top priority. The building is equipped with modern security features such as CCTV surveillance, secure access control systems, and trained security personnel.

    The apartment also has a property management office on-site, which provides residents with additional support and assistance. The property management office is staffed by professionals who are responsible for the smooth operation and maintenance of the building. They handle issues such as maintenance and repairs, queries and general upkeep of the property.

    Overall, the apartment's emphasis on security and the presence of a property management office on-site provide residents with a sense of security and comfort, knowing that their safety and well-being are a top priority.

  • Roof Playground
    and Terrace

    The apartment is designed to provide residents with a comfortable and enjoyable living space, and one of the standout features is the roof playground terrace. This unique outdoor space is located on the roof of the building and provides residents with a safe and fun area to relax, socialize and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding area.

    The area is also designed with safety in mind, with soft and durable flooring, making it ideal for young children to run and play. Parents can relax and enjoy a cup of coffee on the terrace while keeping an eye on their children.

    For individuals who enjoy spending time outdoors, the playground terrace is an excellent place to relax and soak up the sun. The terrace is spacious, providing plenty of room to lounge or socialize with friends and family. It also offers breathtaking views of the surrounding area, providing a sense of peace and tranquility.

  • Three Level Basement Parking

    The apartment is designed to provide residents with ample parking space, making it convenient and secure for them to park their vehicles. The building boasts a state-of-the-art three-level basement parking system, which can accommodate a large number of cars. This is especially beneficial for residents who own multiple cars or have guests who need parking space.

    The parking system is designed to be easy to use, with ample space for cars to maneuver and park. The parking spaces are wide and well-marked, ensuring that residents can park their cars comfortably and without any hassle.

    Overall, the three-level basement parking system is a standout feature of the apartment, providing residents with a convenient and secure place to park their vehicles.
