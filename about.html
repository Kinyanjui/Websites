      About The Project

      Perfect Location
      In The Serene Area of King'eero-Lower Kabete

      Our apartments are strategically located close to reputable schools, hospitals, supermarkets, public transportation as well as the Western and Southern Bypass.

      There is easy access to public transportation: King`eero is located along lower kabete road and is a major transportation hub for buses, matatus, and motorcycles. This makes it easy for residents to access other parts of Kiambu County and Nairobi.

      It`s proximity which is home to several shopping centers, including the Wangige Market, which offers a wide range of fresh produce, clothes, and household items. Other nearby shopping centers include the Mountain Mall, which features a cinema, supermarket, and several shops.

      Educational institutions: There are several primary and secondary schools located in and around Wangige, including the Wangige Primary School and the Wangige Girls High School. Health facilities: Wangige is home to several healthcare facilities, including the Wangige Health Centre, which offers a range of medical services to the local community.

      Natural attractions: The town is surrounded by several hills, including the Ndumbuini Hill and the Wangige Hill, which offer panoramic views of the surrounding areas. The Karura Forest, which is a popular destination for nature walks, bird watching, and picnics, is also located nearby.

      Security & Managment Office

      The safety and security of residents are of utmost importance in the design of this apartment. To ensure this, the building is equipped with a comprehensive security system that includes CCTV surveillance cameras strategically placed throughout the premises, secure access control systems that restrict entry to authorized personnel only, and trained security personnel who are available around the clock to monitor and respond to any security concerns.

      The apartment also has a property management office on-site, which provides residents with additional support and assistance. The property management office is staffed by professionals who are responsible for the smooth operation and maintenance of the building.

      They handle issues such as maintenance and repairs, collection of rent, and general upkeep of the property. Residents can access the property management office for any issues related to the apartment or the building, including security concerns.

      The office is open during regular business hours and has emergency protocols in place to handle urgent matters outside of regular hours.

      Overall, the apartment's emphasis on security and the presence of a property management office on-site provide residents with a sense of security and comfort, knowing that their safety and well-being are a top priority.

      Sufficient Water and Power Supply

      The apartment is equipped with a reliable and ample supply of water to ensure that residents have uninterrupted access to this essential service.

      Water is sourced from multiple sources, including a borehole, which provides a steady supply of clean water even during periods of water shortages. The building also has an underground storage tank that stores large quantities of water to ensure a constant supply of water for residents.

      In addition to the borehole and storage tank, the apartment has overhead water tanks that provide additional storage and ensure a constant supply of water even during times of high demand.

      Similarly, the apartment has a reliable and uninterrupted power supply, with a stand-by generator as a backup power source. This ensures that residents have access to power even during periods of power outages or load shedding.

      The generator is designed to automatically kick in as soon as the main power supply goes out, ensuring that there is no disruption to residents' daily activities. Overall, the apartment's robust water and power supply systems provide residents with peace of mind, knowing that they will always have access to these essential services.

      Roof Playground Terrace &
      Three Level Basement Parking

      The apartment is designed to provide residents with a comfortable and enjoyable living & parking space.

      One of the standout features is the roof gym/playground terrace. This unique outdoor space is located on the roof of the building and provides residents with a safe and fun area to relax, socialize and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding area.

      The area is also designed with safety in mind, with soft and durable flooring, making it ideal for young children to run and play. Parents can relax and enjoy a cup of coffee on the terrace while keeping an eye on their children.

      The apartment is also designed to provide residents with ample parking space, making it convenient and secure for them to park their vehicles.

      The building boasts a three-level basement parking space, which can accommodate a large number of cars. This is especially beneficial for residents who own multiple cars or have guests who need parking space.
